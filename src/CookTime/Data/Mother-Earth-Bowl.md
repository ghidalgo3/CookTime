# Mother Earth Bowl

\rcAuthorSymbol{} By
: goodnesswithg, \url{https://goodnesswithg.com/homemade-flower-child-mother-earth-bowl/}

\rcClockSymbol{} Ready in
: 60 minutes

\rcServingSymbol{} Serves
: 2

## Ingredients

- 4 oz sweet potatoes, roasted
- 6 oz broccoli florets, roasted
- 5 oz portobello mushroom, sauteed
- 1  zucchini, sauteed
- 8 oz arugula
- 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
- 12 oz grains mix (quinoa, farro)
- 1/2 avocado, sliced

## Directions
#. Pre-heat oven to 400F. Roast sweet potatoes and broccoli with olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices to taste, about 30 minutes.
#. Sautee mushrooms and zucchinis with olive oil, salt, pepper, and spices to taste, about 15 minutes.
#. Cook grains according to packaging instructions.
#. Toss arugula in rice wine vinegar, add to a bowl.
#. Serve sweet potatoes, broccoli, mushroom, zucchini, and arugula with the grains and avocado slices.
